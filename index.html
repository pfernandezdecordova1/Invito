Our Story

The World Doesn't Wait.
Neither Do We.

INVITO was born in the summer of 2019 inside a cramped apartment in downtown Barcelona, when three college friends threw a rooftop party that was never supposed to happen. Forty strangers showed up through word of mouth alone. A retired diplomat danced next to a street musician. A tech founder shared a drink with a philosophy student. By sunrise, half the guests had exchanged numbers and the other half had already planned the next gathering. That night proved something we had always suspected: the right room changes everything. We turned that instinct into a platform. Within a year, INVITO orchestrated "La Nuit Dorée" in a private château outside Paris — 200 guests, zero public advertising, and a waiting list of 1,400. We followed it with "The Obsidian Affair" on a yacht off the coast of Mykonos, where founders, artists, and diplomats mingled under the stars. Our Havana chapter, "Club de Humo," fused live jazz, vintage cigars, and candlelit courtyards, selling out in eleven minutes. Each event different, each one unforgettable.

Today, INVITO operates across 14 cities on four continents. We are not an events company — we are a connections engine. Every gathering we curate is designed to place you in a room where opportunity, culture, and conversation collide. Whether you are a young professional seeking mentors in New York, a creative looking for collaborators in Tokyo, or simply someone who refuses to settle for ordinary nights out in London — this is where you belong. Our members have closed business deals over cocktails in Milan, found lifelong friends at a masquerade in Buenos Aires, and launched startups that trace their origin to a single INVITO introduction. We don't just throw parties. We architect the moments that reshape the trajectory of your life. If you believe the people you surround yourself with define your future, then stop scrolling and start living. The next chapter of your story begins with one invitation.
